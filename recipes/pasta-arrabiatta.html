Pasta Arrabiata

Description

The spicy italian pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 250g Rigatoni
  • 1 liter of water
  • Salt
  • Pancetta
  • One can of chopped tomatoes
  • Chili flakes

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in the water
  2. Fry pancetta, onion, garlic and chili flakes
  3. Add tomatoes and cook out the fluids
  4. Mix the pasta in the sauce, and add some parmesan
